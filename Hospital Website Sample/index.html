OUR MISSION

Our mission is to be a center of excellence and provide world class quality health care with dignity through a humane and tender approach as is depicted in our logo where two tender hands are holding a human being and giving care with dignity. We would like to maintain the highest possible standard for medical care..

OUR VISION

Our vision is to match the existing Super Specialty Hospitals in both the public and private sectors and be a leading Super-Specialty Tertiary Care Center ourselves. We aim to incorporate state-of-the-art scientific & technological advances in health care, teaching, training and research activities which can help in the better management of patients and updating the knowledge and skills of the health care providers.

Welcome To Janakpuri Super Speciality Hospital Society !

The Hospital has been constructed on anÂ area of 8.82 Acres. Total covered area: 36,000 sq. meters. The Hospital was inaugurated by the then Honorable Chief Minister of Delhi on 18thÂ Sept., 2008. The Janakpuri Super Specialty Hospital (JSSH) was registered as Society on 20th September, 2013. At present the OPD services in Cardiology, Gastroentrology, Neurology , Nephrology andÂ supportive services of Laboratory (Hematology, Cytology, Biochemistry, Microbiology), Radiology, Facility Integrated Testing and Counselling Centre (F-ICTC for HIV testing), Integrated Rehabilitation center (Speech therapy, Occupational and Physiotherapy), Cardiodiagnostic, Gastrodiagnostic and Neurodiagnostic zone are operational. Janakpuri Super Specialty Hospital (JSSH) has a provision of 250 beds.

Message from Managing Director

Janakpuri Super Specialty hospital Society is envisaged to be a leading super specialty health care provider. We are very new, still in our infancy, starting to take our first steps but we will soon be walking on our feet and will provide superior unparalleled quality health care services.
